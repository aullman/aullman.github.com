OpenTok - Session basics

  • Session
    • properties
      • sessionId - unique id for this session, like a room id.
      • connection - describes your connection to the session
    • methods
      • connect(apiKey, token) - Connect to the session
      • disconnect() - Disconnect from the session
      • publish():Publisher - Publish your video to the Session
      • unpublish(publisher) - Stop publishing your stream.
      • subscribe(stream):Subscriber - Subscribe to someone's stream
      • unsubscribe(subscriber) - Unsubscribe from someone's stream
    • events
      • sessionConnected - You successfully connected to the session.
      • sessionDisconnected - You have disconnected from the session.
      • streamCreated - Someone started publishing a new stream in the session.
      • streamDestroyed - Someone stopped publishing.
      • connectionCreated - Someone connected to the session.
      • connectionDestroyed - Someone disconnected from the session.
